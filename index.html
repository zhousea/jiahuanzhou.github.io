About Me

I received my Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Ying Wu from Northwestern University in 2018. Before coming to Northwestern, I have received my B.E. degree in the Automation Department from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China in 2013. Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Fellow working with Prof.Ying Wu in Northwestern University. My research interests include computer vision and pattern recognition, machine learning and data mining.
